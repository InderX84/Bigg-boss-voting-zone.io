Who We Are

Welcome to the Bigg Boss Voting Zone — a platform for fans to engage, share opinions, and vote for their favourite contestants. We are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about bringing you the most accurate and up-to-date information about the show, its contestants, and the voting process.

How to Vote

  1. Create an account using your email address.
  2. Browse the list of active contestants on the home page.
  3. Click the Vote button next to your favourite contestant.
  4. Your vote is recorded instantly — each account gets one vote per round.
  5. Voting windows open after each episode and close within a limited time frame.
